About Us



Welcome to Autumn Designs—your go-to pseudo-designing company! We pride ourselves on our unique knack for questionable design choices and brilliantly half-baked ideas. While we might not be the best in the business, we promise to cook up something that’s at least a step up—just hire us, and we’ll sprinkle some magic (or maybe just some glitter) on your project!


Big thanks to all the resource wizards who helped turn our wild ideas into something semi-coherent! Let’s create some magic together—preferably without any clipart cats!


If you like what you see and are looking for a project to collaborate on, don’t be shy—contact me! I’m excited to turn your ideas into reality!
